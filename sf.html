Zeqi in SF

My Bay Area Memoir

Friday, Nov 17, 2023
It's been 4 months since I've moved out of the Bay Area. Never thought I'd develop a love-hate relationship with this place but here we are. Truthfully told, I've never been here for longer than 5 months at a time. I was either interning or visiting home for a couple of weeks or months in between.

The Bay was a place I always considered beautiful. I never got tired of driving the 101 North and watching the South San Francisco sign appear. I loved its proximity to nature, the eclectic Victorian architechture and how colourful and intense the sunsets were. But the more I settled in, I began to feel a restlessness, a sense that there was something more out there. Living in South Bay didn't help but the city wasn't too inspiring either when you'd be out-of-luck finding anything open past 9pm. Ultimately, I moved. A pretty common theme in my life for the past 8 years, but this time it's different: to a city that's closer to home, both culturally and geographically. Will I find that here in New York? Maybe, but it's 7pm now and I should probably go to dinner soon.
